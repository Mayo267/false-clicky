Led Zeppelin Tribute Page

Led Zeppelin is a band that formed in 1968 when Jimmy Page (Right Center) invited Robert Plant (Right) and John Bonham (Left Center) to join his band, the New Yardbirds. In October of 1968 they took the name Led Zeppelin based on a humorous conversation among several musicians about their chances of going down like a lead balloon.
